Memory Snap
The Memory Snap game is a fun and engaging card-matching game that tests players' memory and reflexes. It involves a deck of shuffled cards, usually with pairs of matching images, numbers, or symbols. Players take turns flipping two cards at a time, trying to find matching pairs. If a player finds a match, they keep the pair and take another turn; otherwise, the cards are turned back over.
